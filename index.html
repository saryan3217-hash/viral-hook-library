How to Use These Hooks

3-Step Hook Formula

The formula every viral creator uses, simplified.

01
Start With the Hook

The first 1–3 seconds must stop the scroll. Use one of these hooks as your opening line — spoken or as text on screen.

02
Create Curiosity

After the hook, tease the value. Don't give everything away immediately. Keep them watching by building tension.

03
Deliver the Value

Keep your promise. The hook created expectation — now deliver something genuinely useful, surprising, or emotional.
